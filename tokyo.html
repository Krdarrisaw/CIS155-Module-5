My Tokyo Dream Trip

Tokyo has been on my travel list for a long time. It feels like one of those cities where you can experience both modern life and traditional culture in the same day. I’m especially interested in the food, the technology, and the overall vibe of the city.

What I Want to Do in Tokyo

  • Visit Shibuya Crossing and explore nearby shops.
  • Try authentic ramen and sushi.
  • See the view from Tokyo Tower.
  • Walk around Harajuku and check out the fashion.
  • Visit Senso-ji Temple and learn some history.
