Tokyo Trip Plans

Why Tokyo?

Tokyo feels like a mix of the future and deep tradition. Neon signs, quiet temples, arcades, and tiny ramen shops all on the same street.

Must-See Spots

  • Shibuya Crossing
  • Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa
  • Akihabara for games and anime

Food & Vibes

I want to try ramen, okonomiyaki, konbini snacks, and see what late-night life looks like in Shinjuku or Shibuya.

I’m also curious about the quieter neighborhoods—small cafes, side streets, and parks where locals actually spend time.
