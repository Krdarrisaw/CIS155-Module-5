My Paris Dream Trip

Paris is a place I’ve always wanted to see in person. I’ve heard so much about the architecture, museums, and cafés. It seems like the kind of city where simply walking around would be enjoyable.

Things I Want to Do in Paris

  • See the Eiffel Tower and take photos from different angles.
  • Visit the Louvre Museum.
  • Try French pastries like croissants and macarons.
  • Walk along the Seine River.
  • Relax at a café and people-watch.
